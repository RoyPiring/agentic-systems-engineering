## What this domain covers

AI and ML engineering across agents, multi-agent orchestration, model integration, and self-applied daily-driver tooling. Includes forges, evaluation harnesses, and the meta-tooling that produces other systems.

**What it isn't.** A guarantee of agent reliability under all conditions. A vendor-neutral comparison of AI frameworks.
